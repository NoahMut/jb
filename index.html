About RBH Relief

Our Services

OCD

Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) is a mental health issue characterized by persistent intrusive thoughts and repetitive actions known as compulsions. While OCD can be relatively benign, it often disrupts everyday tasks, work responsibilities, and interpersonal connections.

Anxiety

Fear, worry, uneasiness, or stress caused due to anxiety issues can be controlled when there is treatment available for help.

ADD/ADHD

ADHD (Attention-Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder), and ADD (Attention Deficit Disorder) are mental health conditions that makes it difficult to focus on work and school life.

Depression

If you find yourself consistently experiencing low moods and disengagement from activities and relationships you once enjoyed, you might be dealing with depression.

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder is A disorder associated with episodes of mood swings ranging from depressive lows to manic highs.

PTSD

PTSD, known as Post Traumatic Stress Disorder, is a psychological condition where individuals struggle to recover after being exposed to or experiencing a deeply distressing event. The lingering impact of such trauma can be profound and persistent.

Substance Abuse

A person is under the influence of Substance Use if they do Excessive use of psychoactive drugs, such as alcohol, pain medications, or illegal drugs. And excess use of this substance can lead to physical, social, or emotional harm.

Medication Management

Medication management is essential for patients suffering from mental health problems or psychiatric issues to help them cope with symptoms.
